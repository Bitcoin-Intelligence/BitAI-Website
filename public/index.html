HOW IT WORKS

The First Agentic P2P Platform

Local AGI Network

The Local AGI network is powered by high-performance devices running the Local AGI desktop app. These devices provide intelligence for the entire platform, allowing less powerful devices to utilize Local AGI agents when they lack the resources to run local AI models.

Desktop App

The user-friendly app that simplifies the multi-agent system's complexity, enabling anyone to effortlessly delegate complex real-world tasks to Local AGI agents.

Local AI Agents

Local AGI agents operate locally within a secure sandbox, easing concerns about scale, privacy and security while preventing data errors caused by AI hallucination.

Local AI Models

Local AI Models provide sovereign privacy (as data always stays on local device), unlimited usage, and consistent performance.
